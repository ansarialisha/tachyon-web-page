How does

it work?

Deploying cache on the edge improves website speed by bringing content closer to your users. Edge servers store cached data in locations geographically near your customers, reducing the distance data must travel. This minimizes latency and accelerates load times, providing a faster experience for visitors accessing your website.

Smart

Routing

Implementing smart routing and invalidation algorithms ensures your content is refreshed only when it becomes stale. This intelligent system monitors your content's freshness and updates it precisely when necessary, eliminating unnecessary data transfers. As a result, it reduces server load and bandwidth usage.

Pro Level

Security

By integrating pro-level security features like Web Application Firewalls (WAF) and custom firewall rules, your cached content is protected against a wide range of cyber threats. The WAF acts as a barrier between your website and the internet, monitoring incoming traffic and blocking malicious activities such as SQL injections, cross-site scripting, and other attacks.

Sale Mode

ON

By enabling SALE MODE, your content is cached and deployed to nodes closer to your customers, giving your website a turbo speed boost. Especially beneficial during peak traffic like sales or promotions, this strategy reduces latency and ensures fast load times regardless of user location. The result is a seamless user experience that handles high traffic without compromising performance.
